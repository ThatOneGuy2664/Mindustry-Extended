# What is this game then?
You are the Sharded, a new faction emerging from Erekir and Serpulo, every new planet you go to has a vast variety of resources. New technology must be developed for each of them. Experiment with early iterations of tech, futuristic machines, and so much more. There are currently two full planets, with a third WIP as of now. Think of it as Mindustry II. Lore has been made for the game, and is being implemented.
