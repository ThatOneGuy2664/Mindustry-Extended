# What is this game then?
You are the Sharded, a new faction emerging from Erekir, (Serpulo is not a cannon planet, however feel free to play it for a classic Mindustry experience) every new planet you go to has a vast variety of resources. New technology must be developed for each of them. Expierment with early iterations, futuristic machines, and so much more. There are currently two full planets, with a third WIP as of now.
